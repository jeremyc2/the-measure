## Lower-spoiler / safer context

| Source | Use | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Nikki Erlick author page | Author/publisher-facing context, praise, official links | Low-to-medium; marketing pages can still reveal premise details |

| TODAY discussion questions | Discussion prompts and themes | Medium-to-high; questions assume broader reading |
| Author interviews, e.g. Write or Die / The Gloss / AARP | Authorial intent, pandemic context, themes | Medium; interviewer may discuss full-book arcs |
