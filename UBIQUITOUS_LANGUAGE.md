## Terms

### Essay

Preferred public-facing and implementation term for a written response to _The Measure_. Use **essay** instead of **post**, **blog post**, or **article** in reader-facing copy, planning language, routes, schemas, services, and frontend model names.

### Reading dossier

A spoiler-controlled research workspace for tracking characters, plot points, themes, quotes, questions, and essay seeds within a declared reading scope.

### Reading scope

The explicit boundary for what the reader has reached in the book. e.g. current chapter. 

### Spoiler ledger

A holding area for research findings that may exceed the current reading scope. Items from the spoiler ledger must not appear in essays or conversational summaries unless confirmed to be inside the reading scope.

## Relationships

- An **essay** may draw from the **reading dossier**.
- The **reading dossier** is bounded by the current **reading scope**.
- The **spoiler ledger** may contain material outside the **reading scope** and must be filtered before drafting an **essay**.
